About The Museum

Vision To be the best Museum in Malaysia under the Terengganu State Museum Board.
Mission To serve as a conservation and reference center for local wisdom through creative and innovative practices, serving stakeholders and customers.

Objectives Of The Museum

Objectives Of The Museum
1. To preserve historical and cultural heritage by collecting, preserving, and exhibiting collections of historical value.
2. To conduct research and disseminate information related to Terengganu's culture and history.
3. To promote conservation efforts for local fauna, flora, and rocks.
4. To research and conserve historical monuments and sites throughout Terengganu.
5. To contribute to the development of tourism in the state and nation.
